Rewires

Electrical Rewires

Older properties often have wiring that no longer meets modern safety standards — rubber-insulated cables, inadequate earthing, or no RCD protection. A full rewire gives your home a fresh, fully compliant electrical system.

We carry out domestic rewires across Northampton with minimal disruption, keeping you informed at every stage of the process.

Consumer Units

Fuse Box Upgrades

An old-style fuse board with ceramic fuses or early MCBs does not offer the protection that modern electrics require. A new consumer unit with RCDs and RCBOs significantly improves your home's electrical safety.

We replace outdated fuse boards with modern consumer units across Northampton, typically completing the work within a day.

Fault Finding

Electrical Fault Finding

Tripping RCDs, flickering lights, a circuit that won't stay on, or partial power loss — electrical faults can be frustrating and worrying. We diagnose the root cause efficiently using proper testing equipment.

We respond promptly to urgent faults across Northampton, including situations like damaged mains cabling that need immediate professional attention.
