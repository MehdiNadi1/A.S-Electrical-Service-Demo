Who We Are

Northampton's Dependable Electrician

A.S Electrical Services is a local electrical business based in Northampton. We work with homeowners and small businesses across Northamptonshire, providing a wide range of domestic and light commercial electrical services.

Our approach is simple: turn up on time, do the work to a high standard, keep the site clean, and communicate clearly throughout. We're not the largest electrical company in the county, and that's by design — being a focused local business means every customer matters, and every job gets proper attention.

We've built a reputation in Northampton for being responsive, capable, and calm under pressure. Whether it's a planned lighting installation or an urgent electrical fault that needs resolving the same day, we approach every situation the same way — methodically and professionally.

How We Work

Our Approach to Electrical Work

Safety First

Every installation and repair is carried out in line with UK wiring regulations. We don't cut corners, we don't rush, and we always leave your home or business electrically safe.

Quality Workmanship

From neat cable runs to flush-fitted sockets and tidily wired consumer units — the finish matters to us. We take care with every detail, not just the functional parts of the work.

Clear Communication

We explain what we're doing and why, give honest quotes without hidden extras, and confirm appointments in advance. You'll always know where things stand.

Responsive to Urgent Situations

Electrical emergencies — like severed mains cables, complete power loss, or dangerous faults — receive prompt attention. We respond quickly and work calmly to resolve the problem.

Genuinely Local

We're not a national call centre that sends different engineers each time. We're a local Northampton business — local knowledge, faster response, and a consistent face on the job.

Domestic & Small Commercial

We work with homeowners, landlords, and small businesses. Whether it's a domestic rewire or an electrical upgrade for a local commercial property, we handle it properly.
